Fill In The Blank

  1. Each individual question must be separated by a ~ (except after the last one).
  2. Within each question, there will be a prompt and an answer
  3. The prompt is what will appear on the front of the flash card
  4. The answer is what will appear on the back of the flash card
  5. The answer must be contained within a pair of | (aka pipes)
  6. If you find a mistake upon preview, simply edit the text box again and resubmit

Example input:

On the remote icy planet of Hoth, Han Solo described a |tauntaun| as not only smelly on the outside, but also on the inside. ~
One does not simply walk into |Mordor|. Its Black Gates are guarded by more than just Orcs.

Example output:

Prompt: On the remote icy planet of Hoth, Han Solo described a _____ as not only smelly on the outside, but also on the inside.

Answer: tauntaun

Prompt: One does not simply walk into _____. Its Black Gates are guarded by more than just Orcs.

Answer: Mordor

  1. Each individual question contains 2 input fields: TERM and DEFINITION.
  2. When printing, each page can fit 5 flash cards.
  3. You don't have to fill in all 5 in order to print.
  4. If you find a mistake upon preview, simply edit the fields again and resubmit

Custom

  1. Each individual question must be separated by a ~ (except after the last one).
  2. The front side and the back side must be separated by one | (pipe)
  3. If you find a mistake upon preview, simply edit the text box again and resubmit

Example:
This is the front of the first flash card. |
This is the back of the first flash card. ~
This is the front of the second flash card. |
This is the back of the second flash card.
